Slide Types

Nine slide types total. Cover (always first) and CTA (always last) are fixed. The writer selects 2–6 middle slides from the remaining seven. Each subsection below documents when to use the type, its required fields, word limits, and layout on the 1080×1350 canvas.

Cover fixed — always first

When to use
Always present. Sets story context and brand. Image-first.
Required fields
headline  ·  category  ·  image_query
Word limits
headline: max 12 words  ·  category: max 4 words (shown in uppercase)  ·  image_query: 5–8 words (OG scrape search or FLUX prompt)
Layout
NEAR_BLACK background with bottom-heavy gradient overlay (38→217 alpha, t^1.6 curve). Full-bleed cover image pasted first if image_obj provided; dark solid otherwise. Wordmark "The Frameshift" top-left at (66, 66), DM Sans Bold 45px, WHITE. Headline in bottom third: EB Garamond Regular, starts at 96px, shrinks in 4px steps until block fits ~330px zone; left-aligned from x=66. Category pill below headline: DM Sans Medium 28px, rounded border in lavender. Accent rule with right-pointing arrow at y = H−66, full width.

Stat single number that reframes the story

When to use
Use when one number changes how the reader sees everything else. Not for generic stats.
Required fields
eyebrow  ·  number  ·  unit  ·  context
Word limits
eyebrow: max 4 words  ·  number: e.g. "$14B" or "73%" (min 1 char)  ·  unit: max 8 words  ·  context: max 35 words
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header (wordmark + rule at y=115). Eyebrow (DM Sans Bold 30px, LABEL) at y=165. Giant number (EB Garamond Bold 260px, NEAR_BLACK) below eyebrow. Unit (DM Sans Regular 42px, MUTED) below number. Context body (DM Sans Regular 45px, BODY) below unit. Bottom accent bar: 9px tall lavender strip pinned to bottom edge.

Quote direct quote > paraphrase

When to use
Use when a source's exact words carry more authority or impact than paraphrase.
Required fields
text  ·  attribution
Word limits
text: max 40 words  ·  attribution: free form — format: Name, Title — Organisation
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header. Left accent line: x=66, top=204, bottom=H−200, width=6px, ACCENT colour. Decorative opening quote mark: EB Garamond Regular 210px, QUOTE_MARK_COLOR, at (102, 195) — purely decorative, not layout-bearing. Quote text starts at y=310, inner_x=102: EB Garamond Italic 96px, DARK, wrapped to slide width minus padding. Attribution (DM Sans Medium 42px, MUTED) below quote, same x.

Context what happened + optional image

When to use
Use when the reader needs to know what happened before any analysis or implication lands.
Required fields
kicker  ·  headline  ·  body  ·  use_image
Word limits
kicker: max 3 words  ·  headline: max 15 words  ·  body: max 40 words  ·  use_image: boolean
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header. Kicker (DM Sans Bold 30px, ACCENT_DARK, uppercase) at y=150. Headline (EB Garamond Regular 86px, NEAR_BLACK) below kicker. Image zone (408px tall, full width minus padding) only when use_image=true AND image_obj is available — never shows a grey placeholder. Body copy (DM Sans Regular 37px, BODY) below headline or image zone.

Contrast two competing things as core tension

When to use
Use when the story is fundamentally about two opposing models, approaches, or positions.
Required fields
kicker  ·  label_a  ·  value_a  ·  label_b  ·  value_b  ·  takeaway
Word limits
kicker: max 3 words  ·  label_a/b: max 3 words  ·  value_a/b: max 20 words  ·  takeaway: max 25 words
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header. Eyebrow (DM Sans Bold 30px, LABEL) at y=150. Two side-by-side cells, each ~(W−PAD×2−24)/2 wide, 330px tall, y below eyebrow. Cell A: CONTRAST_A (near-black) background, rounded 9px, no border; label DM Sans Bold 27px in #666, value EB Garamond Regular 54px in WHITE. Cell B: CONTRAST_B background, CONTRAST_B_BORDER outline 2px, rounded 9px; label DM Sans Bold 27px in #bbb, value EB Garamond Regular 54px in NEAR_BLACK. Takeaway (DM Sans Regular 42px, #444) below cells.

Timeline sequence and momentum matter

When to use
Use when the order of events — and the momentum they create — is the story.
Required fields
kicker  ·  events (array of {date, event})
Word limits
kicker: max 3 words  ·  events[].date: free form (year or date string)  ·  events[].event: max 12 words
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header. Kicker (DM Sans Bold 30px, LABEL) at y=150. Events stacked vertically: dot at x=PAD+12, text at x=PAD+48. Dot radius=12px; last event dot is NEAR_BLACK, earlier dots are ACCENT_DARK. Date (DM Sans Bold 30px, same colour as dot) to the right of dot. Event text (DM Sans Regular 37px, DARK) below date. Connector line (RULE_COLOR, 3px) between events, from bottom of dot to below last event text.

Explainer reader needs a concept before the point lands

When to use
Use when the reader needs a concept or term defined before the story's implication makes sense.
Required fields
kicker  ·  question  ·  answer
Word limits
kicker: max 3 words  ·  question: max 15 words  ·  answer: max 55 words
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header. Kicker (DM Sans Bold 30px, LABEL) at y=150. Question (EB Garamond Regular 86px, NEAR_BLACK) below kicker — same treatment as context headline. Horizontal divider (RULE_COLOR, 3px) below question. Answer (DM Sans Regular 42px, #3a3a3a) below divider. Any **bold** markers in the answer are stripped before rendering.

Signal one forward-looking implication

When to use
Use for a single forward-looking implication — what this development means for the next 12–36 months.
Required fields
kicker  ·  headline  ·  implication  ·  tag
Word limits
kicker: max 3 words  ·  headline: max 15 words  ·  implication: max 45 words  ·  tag: max 4 words (min 1 char)
Layout
OFF_WHITE background. Content header. Kicker (DM Sans Bold 30px, ACCENT_DARK, uppercase) at y=150. Headline (EB Garamond Regular 126px, NEAR_BLACK) — largest headline in the palette. Implication (DM Sans Regular 45px, BODY) below headline. Tag pill (SIGNAL_TAG_BG background, SIGNAL_TAG_BORDER outline, ACCENT_DARK text, DM Sans Bold 30px, radius=27px) below implication.

CTA fixed — always last

When to use
Always present. Story-specific follow hook. Drives audience to follow the account.
Required fields
headline
Word limits
headline: max 20 words, story-specific (not generic) — min 10 chars
Layout
Pure BLACK background. Fully centered layout. Rule above handle: 240px wide, WHITE at 25% opacity, y = center_y−180. Handle "@ai.xr.frameshift": DM Sans Regular 33px, #737373, centered. Headline: EB Garamond Bold 102px, WHITE, centered, multi-line wrapping to W−PAD×3. Headline block starts ~60px below center.

Updating Baselines

Regression baselines live at tests/regression/baselines/ — one locked PNG per slide type. They are pixel-compared on every test run. When a design change is intentional, update the baselines before committing.

Regression tests will fail after any renderer or design_constants change until baselines are updated. This is expected — it forces the change to be visible and deliberate.
  1. Edit watcher/design_constants.py if changing a design value
  2. Edit watcher/renderer.py if changing layout or rendering logic
  3. Run make update-baselines to regenerate all 9 baseline PNGs
  4. Visually inspect the new baselines — open them in Finder or Preview
  5. Run make regression and confirm all 18 tests pass
  6. Commit tests/regression/baselines/ alongside the code change
  7. Update this document if any pixel value, colour, or font size changed
